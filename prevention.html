How to protect yourself from E-Frauds



    How do people get scammed? -

  • EMAILS are the most typical form of scam. You'll get emails about you winning a lottery or about investment, tax etc. They'll ask you for your bank details.


  • In exchange for products, services, money, or prizes that they never deliver, a con artist seeks upfront costs or personal information.


  • Scammers frequently use phishing emails to deceive you into giving them access to your computer. They entice you to click on a link or attachment in order to "fish" for your personal information.


    • 1.Use smart and verified apps

      2. Be vigilant when using your card

      3. Never give your passwords

      4. When transferring money use reliable firms

      5. Never transfer or receive money for someone else
